About Us

Welcome to IAS Sourcing Hub, the forefront provider of garment sourcing and supply chain management services in Bangladesh, the global largest apparel producing country. With a relentless dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction, we cater to garment buyers, fashion brand retailers, and wholesalers worldwide. Our journey began with a commitment to revolutionize the garment sourcing landscape by offering tailored Sourcings to meet the evolving needs of our clients. Today, we stand as a beacon of innovation and efficiency in Bangladesh's dynamic apparel industry. At IAS Sourcing Hub, we boast a team of seasoned professionals with extensive expertise in garment manufacturing, sourcing, and supply chain management. Leveraging our vast network of trusted suppliers and partners, we provide end-to-end Sourcings, from product development and manufacturing to logistics and distribution. Transparency, integrity, and reliability are the cornerstones of our business philosophy. We prioritize open communication and strive to build long-lasting partnerships based on trust and mutual respect. Our clients can rely on us to deliver on our promises with unwavering commitment.

SUSTAINABILITY

Sustainability

At IAS Sourcing Hub, sustainability is not just a buzzword; it's a core value ingrained in every aspect of our operations. As a leading provider of garment sourcing and supply chain management services in Bangladesh, we recognize our responsibility to minimize environmental impact and promote ethical practices throughout the apparel industry. We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint by implementing eco-friendly manufacturing processes, utilizing renewable energy sources, and adopting sustainable materials wherever possible. From sourcing organic cotton to investing in water-saving technologies, we strive to conserve resources and minimize waste at every stage of production. Furthermore, we prioritize fair labor practices and ensure that workers in our supply chain are treated ethically and receive fair wages. By fostering a culture of transparency and accountability, we aim to create a more sustainable and socially responsible supply chain. At IAS Sourcing Hub, sustainability is not just a goal; it's a journey that we are committed to pursuing every day, for the betterment of our planet and future generations..
